Contact Us

Hello! We'd love to hear from you, so take a look at the different ways you can get in touch.

Join the forum

If you're keen to talk about Alfred, workflows and features with other users, join the Alfred forum.

You can also get support with workflows on the forum if you haven't found your answers in the Workflows section of the help site or in the Guides and Tutorials.

Find us online

Say hi on Twitter @alfredapp or on our Facebook page. We love hearing how you use Alfred to be more productive!

Lost your license code?

Take a look at how you can recover your license if you can't find your Powerpack license details.

Reporting a bug?

If you've found a bug, you'll find details on how to report it here.

Press and corporate sales enquiries

If you're looking for more information about Alfred, or would like to purchase a corporate license for your team, drop us a line on and we'll be happy to help.

Still need to get in touch?

It's quickest to look at the online help or contact us via Twitter, but if you're unable to resolve your issue, you can get in touch at . Powerpack users should use their registered email address to get a speedier response.
