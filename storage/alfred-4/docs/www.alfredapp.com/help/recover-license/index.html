How can I recover my license code?

We send licenses by email automatically when you make your purchase. Look for emails from @alfredapp.com, for the "Your Alfred License" or Your Alfred Powerpack License" subject line for the original license email we sent you.

If you can't find your license, please contact us on from the email address you used to register your license and we'll reissue your license code as soon as possible.

Contacting us from an alternative email address will almost certainly delay re-sending your license code.
