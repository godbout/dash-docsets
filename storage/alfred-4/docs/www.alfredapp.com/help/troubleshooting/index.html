Troubleshooting

On occasion, you may find that Alfred isn't behaving exactly as expected. We've created the troubleshooting guides below to help you get to the bottom of the issue.

When reading through the troubleshooting guides, please follow the steps carefully. If you're unable to resolve your issue, please take screenshots and provide sufficient information when contacting us for help.

  • I can't find some or any contacts in Alfred's results
  • How does Alfred sort my default results?
  • How can I search for my browser bookmarks?
  • I'm encountering indexing issues in macOS 10.15 Catalina

Miscellaneous troubleshooting questions

  • I'm having issues buying the Powerpack
  • I've hidden Alfred's menu bar icon and I can't remember my hotkey
  • How can I reset Alfred to default settings?
  • How can I set the keyboard language I want Alfred to use if I use multiple languages or layouts?

  • How can I uninstall Alfred?

If you haven't been able to find an answer to your question, pop by Alfred forum and let us know what your issue is. We'll be happy to help - just be sure to give enough details for us to be able to help you troubleshoot your problem. :)
